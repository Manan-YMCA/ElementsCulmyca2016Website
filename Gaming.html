Rules:

Version to be used: EA SPORTS® FIFA 16

1. Tournament type: Single-player knockout event. Each Player will be playing two games on home and away basis. “Away-goals” rule is applicable.
2. Half-length: 5 min / 6 min for the semi-final and final.
3. In case of a draw after two rounds, the participants play out one half of an exhibition match. In the unlikely case that the draw is still not resolved, the participants play out a penalty-shootout in the practice arena.
4. Pausing the game is allowed only when the ball is out of play, otherwise the defaulter will have to forfeit the match with a 3-0 deficit.
