CULMYCA 2016 | YMCAUST
The event comprises of three rounds, with elimination after each round. The three rounds are:
1. Prelims
2. Buzzer Round
3. Rapid fire round.

RULES:
1. Each team shall have three members.
2. The Prelims round shall consist of a multiple choice question (MCQ) test.
3. Top eight teams will qualify to the buzzer round.
4. Top four teams will qualify to the Rapid Fire round.
5. In the Rapid fire round, each team will get 60 seconds to answer maximum questions.
6. Use of any electronic device like cellphone, Tab, laptop etc will lead to disquaification of the team.
7. The decision of the Judges will be final.
General Rules:
1. Each team shall have 2 members.
2. Members can be from different colleges.
3. No internet access shall be allowed.
4. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
5. A team will be eliminated after every round.
General Rules:
• Each team can have a maximum of 2 participants.
• A team may consist of students from different colleges.
• Teams are not allowed to bring any kind of written material with them.
• No internet access is allowed.
• Any team involved in unfair means such as bringing electronic equipments or written material, helping other teams during the quiz or breaking the rules will be disqualified instantly.
• The organizers reserve the right to change the rules as they deem fit.
• Decision of the judges will be final.

Quiz Structure/Points System:
Round 1: The teams will be asked a set of questions on a projector screen which they will have to answer on the answer sheet provided. Some questions may not contain any option. Three points shall be awarded for a correct answer and 1 point shall be deducted for a wrong answer. The sheets will be evaluated by the organizers. Top 6 scoring teams will proceed to the next round.

Round 2: This round will be ""Answer or Pass"" round. The teams will be given 30 seconds to answer a question. If they answer the question correctly, they will get +20 marks. If the team is unable to answer, the question will be passed on to the next team. +10 marks will be given to the team which answers this question correctly. The order of teams will be according to the marks of 1st round. The team answering out of their turn will be penalized 10 marks. Top 4 teams will proceed to the next round.

Round 3:This round will be the Buzzer round.Questions will be thrown at the teams, the first one to press the buzzer will be allowed to answer. The team will be given 10 seconds to answer the question. +20 marks will be awarded for correct answer. If the team fails to answer correctly, the question will be passed to other teams. The correct one to answer gets +30 marks. Top 2 teams will be selected for round 4.

Round 4: One member from each team will be given a card containing the question. Both teams will be asked the same question. The team member will have to draw the answer to the question or the question itself on a board. The person drawing is not allowed to speak, nor is he allowed to write on the board. The person drawing will only be allowed to confirm the guesses of his team member without speaking. The members are not allowed to point at any object other than what is drawn on the board. The other member will have to guess the answer by looking at the picture. Team members are allowed to look at other teams' drawings. The team to do so first wins.

Tie Breaker: In the event of a tie between 2 teams, bonus questions will be asked. The structure of the tie breaker will be decided at the event.
