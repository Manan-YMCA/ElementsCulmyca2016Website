PRAVEGA 2016 | Indian Institute of Science

Here is another chance to tackle the crisis of environmental damage. An event for the true Chemical Engineer, it is a double-layered test of the participants’ affinity for a radical solution to industrial problems. Whether you atomize the economy or economize the atoms, an element of change is what we are looking for.

Tread the path of Green Chemistry in two exciting rounds:
1) A written test that provokes the Chemist in you.
2) An engrossing group discussion to substitute for existing pathways and come up with more favourable ones.

Rules:
1)Each team can have maximum of three members
2)The first round is general chemistry written quiz for half an hour.
3)5 teams will proceed to the final round.
4)The final round will be a group discussion wherein certain chemical pathways will be given, for which the participant are to identify issues and suggest improvements and alternatives.
5)Estimated time for final round is 3 hours.
6)Pen and paper will be provided. Participants are requested to bring scientific calculators

A palette of chemicals waiting to paint a T-shirt canvas!
Let salts be your reactants, your wit be your catalyst and a colourful T-shirt, your product. Watch while inorganic chemical reactions transform a mundane white T-shirt into an ensemble of colours.

Rules:
1)Each team will have two members.
2)Participants are to synthesize their own colours from inorganic chemicals provided and use them to paint T-shirts.
3)Estimated duration of the event is 3.5 hours.
4)Plain white T-shirts, paintbrushes, pencils, gum(for thickening colours), papers(to write down reactions) and spare cloth will be provided.
5)In case participants are interested to bring their own brushes for the event, they are welcome to do so. However, solely participants would be responsible for any loss or damage to any of their material.
6)Gloves will be provided to all of the participants. In case any participant is found not wearing them, the team to which the participant belongs will be disqualified immediately.
