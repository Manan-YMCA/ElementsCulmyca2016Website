RAKZZ

YMCAUST invites you to express your heart's desire and sway the crowd with your moves. The event has been a platform for dance enthusiasts to showcase their talents and get recognized on the biggest stages. So, come everyone, and entice the audience with your bollywood and western moves.

RULES:

PRELIMS:

DATE: 11th March 2016
TIME: 12:00 PM

1. The maximum time allowed for the performance is 4+2 minutes. (Should be a part of team's final performance.
2. Maximum team size: 20 - there must be at least 6 members on stage at any point during the performance.
3. Free style dance (all kinds of dances allowed).
4. There is restriction on the use of fire or any inflammable object and glass shatter on stage. Negative points may be awarded in such a case.
5. The teams should restore the stage to its previous condition after their performance.
6. The teams must bring pendrives/CDs for the music track (MP3 audio format).
7. Selected teams will go in the final round.

FINALS:

DATE: 11th MARCH 2015
VENUE: Main Stage

1. This round is a free style dancing round in which all kinds of dance are allowed.
2. Maximum time allowed for the performance is 8+4 minutes. Teams exceeding this time will be penalized.
3. A minimum of 6 members must be on stage at any time during the performance.
4. There is restriction on the use of fire or any inflammable object and glass shatter on stage. Negative points may be awarded in such a case.
5. The teams should restore the stage to its previous condition after their performance.
6. The teams must bring pendrives/CDs for the music track (MP3 audio format).
7. Teams can consist of a maximum of 20 members.

Awards :
Handsome cash prize for the winning team.

REGISTRATION :
Last date of registration for the event is 10th March 2016.

MR AND MISS CULMYCA

Unlike its contemporaries, here it is not just about your style quotient or vogue. As they say, "Beauty with brain is an absolutely deadly combination", we believe: "A chivalrous knight is equally lethal!" 
So join in and prove your mettle to get hold of the most coveted title of the fiesta!
The battle for the title will go through a series of 3 competitive rounds.

ROUND I
Register for the contest at: http://ocul.in/faceofculmyca
Fill out the details and other necessary information required.
Submit your photograph that best describes you, with 'culmyca' appearing
anywhere in background. The most creative photographs will be scored higher.
The photographs will be uploaded on our Facebook page where you need to make sure you get  as many likes as possible. Likes on facebook page shall account for 50% of the total score.
Instructions:
1. Applicant must be a student of a recognised institution.
2. Age limit: 18-25 years.
3. Deadline- 11:59PM of 1st March.


ROUND II
Selected candidates from ROUND I will mail a video that showcases their talent in the best way possible. All talents are appreciated. Mail us a glimpse of it at: faceofculmyca@gmail.com.
  Instructions:
1. Deadline-11:59PM of 9th March
2. Video duration should not exceed 60 seconds.

ROUND III
Candidates qualifying ROUNDS I and II will go through a final showdown on-stage where they will face our judges. They will be judged on the basis of a ramp walk and their answers to various tricky hypothetical situations. Buckle up for an exciting face-off.
Instructions: Reach the venue on time.
